Frequently asked questions

Can I attend?

As long as you are 18 years of age or older, you are invited to apply to YorkU hacks.

How does registration work?

The registration will be open soon! You can then complete the application and we will get back to you once we've had the chance to review your application. We will update the status of your application once it's being processed.

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem at all. Hackathon is meant to be a place to pick up new skills and concepts. Ther will be workshops and mentors which will help ease you through the hackathon experience.

How do I get there?

If you live in Toronto or North York, you will have to make your own travel arrangements. Free parking pass will be provided if you choose to drive. Transportation will be provided if you live in Montreal or Waterloo.

What should I bring?

It's best to bring your own laptop, even though you can always borrow a laptop from our hardware lab. Make sure to bring a government issued ID. We will email you a list of everything else you might need during this hackathon (eg: toothbrush, sleeping bag, deodorant, etc)

How does a team work?

Teams consist of 4 to 5 members. If you have your own team you can specify it during the registration process. You can always use our facebook group to find other individuals who might be your potential team members.
