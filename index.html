a festival of ideas

YorkU Hacks isn't just another hackathon. It is a festival to celebrate innovative ideas. We are uniting 300 of the brightest minds from diverse disciplines to tackle some of the global challenges. You’ll plunge into your social conscience and will tackle the problems that plague our world today. Be it climate change, clean water or cyber security, we aim to empower the next 300 to see the opportunities in problems.

In 24 hours time, you will have a unique opportunity to learn and create. You will explore your passions and learn in ways that go beyond the technical. By the end of the hackathon you will end up developing, testing and deploying your ideas created from scratch.

explore

Explore not only your ideas, but your passions. Hacking is more than just technological; it is creative problem solving. At YorkU Hacks, we value diversity in thought which encourages discussion and dissemination of extraordinary ideas. We will foster your curiosity and let you explore all the undiscovered avenues of creativity.

Enveloped by local art, nature, and culture, you will recreate ideas and unmask the connections between the things that we take for granted. Exploring your passions in such a unique way will lead you to the different avenues at this year’s YorkU Hacks.

code

The quintessential hacker experience! Indulge in the new technologies and digest new tricks and skills from our various workshops that cover Machine Learning and App Development and so much more. Hack your way through the weekend as the tech artisan of the bunch.

design

As a designer, you will act as the bridge between the various personas at YUHacks. Intersecting usability, feasibility and viability, you will be the agent for a successful product design. Boost your portfolio with our workshops. Then cap the weekend off with a portfolio that’s been polished by successful designers!

Venture

Develop a startup! Explore your passion in entrepreneurship. Expand your knowledge with our workshops that focus on Project Management, Business Models and Customer Development Models, just to name a few. Launch your startup, win prizes and maybe even make a sale.

Social
Innovation

In order to tackle the challenging problems that we face as a generation, we need the most ambitious and passionate to be thinking about how to solve them.There will be guided workshops for participants to help expand their knowledge in this sector and understand how local solutions can be answers to global problems.

learn + create

At the heart of a 70 year old Computer Science Department and a five year young Lassonde School of Engineering, we are in a unique position to see groundbreaking researches merging with an era of constant change. YorkU Hacks bridges the gap between students with a passion for learning and inspiring industry leaders with a desire to share.

Learning by doing has proven itself to be the most effective ways of imparting knowledge. At YorkU Hacks, we create a space for students to cultivate and to redefine their creative thinking by engaging them practical activties, workshops, and experiences.

Frequently asked questions

Can I attend?

As long as you are 18 years of age or older, you are invited to apply to YorkU hacks.

How does registration work?

The registration will be open soon! You can then complete the application and we will get back to you once we've had the chance to review your application. We will update the status of your application once it's being processed.

What if I don't know how to code?

No problem at all. Hackathon is meant to be a place to pick up new skills and concepts. Ther will be workshops and mentors which will help ease you through the hackathon experience.

How do I get there?

we are situated at a very easy subway from toronto which is connected If you live in Toronto or North York, you will have to make your own travel arrangements. Free parking pass will be provided if you choose to drive.

What should I bring?

It's best to bring your own laptop, even though you can always borrow a laptop from our hardware lab. Make sure to bring a government issued ID. We will email you a list of everything else you might need during this hackathon (eg: toothbrush, sleeping bag, deodorant, etc)

How does a team work?

Teams consist of 4 to 5 members. If you have your own team you can specify it during the registration process. You can always use our facebook group to find other individuals who might be your potential team members.
